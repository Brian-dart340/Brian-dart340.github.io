Recipe #2: Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients Required:

  • Cooking oil
  • Eggs
  • Cold, day old rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Green onions

Instructions:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and beat well and add a pinch of salt
  2. Chop your green onions into small pieces and set aside
  3. Get your wok nice and hot. Use a teaspoon of cooking oil and add it in the wok by swirling it around the wok.
  4. Pour the eggs in and start scrambling. Once they are nearly cooked, add 2 more teaspoons of cooking oil, and then add in the cold, day old rice.
  5. Mix the rice with the egg and then ad in the soy sauce. Be sure to pour in the soy sauce around th edge of the wok for better flavor
  6. Add in the green onions last and continue stirring everything
  7. OPTIONAL: you can add a garnish on top of the egg fried rice like sesame seeds
