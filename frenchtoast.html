Recipe #3: French Toast

Ingredients Required:

  • White bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Add in 2/3 cups of milk, 2 large eggs, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon, and a pinch of salt into a bowl and whisk away
  2. Add a tablespoon of butter into a skillet and turn on the stove for medium-high heat
  3. Coat the slices of white bread by dunking them into the mixture and cook it on the skillet until golden brown for about 3 to 4 minutes per side. After that, serve and enjoy!
